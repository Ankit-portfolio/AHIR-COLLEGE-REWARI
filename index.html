About The Institution

Our Legacy & Mission

Welcome to Ahir College!

Ahir College has a global outlook towards education to match the international standards of education. The College invested a great amount of time to create a well-designed curriculum for each programme that guides students to a bright future. The quality education offered by Ahir College has created a wave among various organisations and honoured with rankings and awards in different categories.


We combine traditional values with modern technology, ensuring our students are not just graduates, but responsible citizens ready for the global workforce.

Message From leaderships

Rao Inderjeet Singh

Hon'ble President

"It gives me immense pleasure to know that Ahir College, Rewari is upgrading the college website from the year 2020. Ahir College is the oldest institution of higher education in southern Haryana as it was founded in 1945. The college has been imparting higher education through various Under Graduate, Post Graduate and Job Oriented courses. Ahir College has a glorious tradition, being committed to excellence in the fields of education as well as sports by employing highly qualified and experienced faculty. I convey my best wishes for the success & prosperity of the students of the past, present & future of this immensely respected institution. "

Dr. Urmila Sharma

Principal

"Education is the most powerful weapon. At Ahir College, we emphasize discipline, research, and practical learning. I welcome all aspiring students to be a part of our academic family.Ahir College, Rewari, was established in 1945 with the mission of spreading knowledge and enlightenment among the youth, both locally and in distant regions, serving as a beacon of education. The college continues to thrive, inspired by the vision and blessings of the esteemed Late Rao Birender Singh Ji, former Union Minister. The college operates under the aegis of Ahir College Society (Regd.) and is supported by a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty, committed to provide value-based education to young minds."
